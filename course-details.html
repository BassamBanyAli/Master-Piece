Website Design: Principles and Practices

This comprehensive course on Website Design provides a deep dive into the principles and practices essential for creating effective and aesthetically pleasing websites. Covering both theoretical and practical aspects, the course is designed to equip students with the skills necessary to design and develop modern, user-friendly websites. Students will explore topics such as responsive design, user experience (UX) principles, and web accessibility, while also gaining hands-on experience with popular design tools and technologies. The course includes practical assignments and projects that allow students to apply what they’ve learned in real-world scenarios, preparing them for careers in web design and development.

Course Objectives

Key learning outcomes and skills students will gain from the course.

By the end of the course, students will be able to design and develop responsive websites, understand user experience principles, and apply web accessibility guidelines. They will also be proficient in using various design tools and techniques to create effective and engaging web pages.
